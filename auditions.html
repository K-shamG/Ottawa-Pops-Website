Auditions

Applicants are accepted into the orchestra based on their audition performance. There is no minimum age requirement for participation in the OPO.

Auditions for oboes, violins, violas and cellos are ongoing for remainder of the season.

The audition will consist of a scale, 2 contrasting pieces and a sight reading. Applicants should select two contrasting pieces that demonstrate their playing level and technical facility. Applicants are asked to arrive 10-15 minutes before their scheduled time and to bring extra copies of their sheet music for the audition panel. All scales are to be played at BPM=60 (minimum) unless otherwise specified, in 16th notes ascending and descending (8 to a bow for strings).

  • Violin, Cello, Flute/Piccolo: D major 3 octaves.
  • Viola: G major 3 octaves.
  • Oboe/English Horn: E-flat major 2 octaves.
  • Clarinet/Bass Clarinet: F major 3 octaves.
  • Brass, Bassoon, & Bass: any 2 octave scale.
  • Percussion (on the Xylophone): Bb major 2 octaves and a third (Bb to D), alternating sticks starting on the left hand. BPM=80.
  • Harp: Any 3 octaves HT, BPM+80.
  • Piano: Ab major parallel motion scale 4 octaves HT, BPM=104. F# minor tonic four note chord broken alternate-note pattern 2 octaves (ending with I-IV-164-V-I chord progression) HT, BPM=80.

Membership Information

All applicants will be informed of the outcome of their audition. The OPO concert season runs from September to mid-May, with weekly rehearsals occurring on Sunday evenings from 6pm to 9pm in room 109 of Perez Hall, 610 Cumberland St. Applicants are asked to ensure they can fully commit to this schedule; members may not miss more than one rehearsal per concert. Applicants must be available for all concerts of the season (December 21, December 22, December 23, March 23, May 17, May 18), including dress rehearsals. All members must re-audition each season.
